Follow Me Down The Rabbit Hole

The king is dead; long live the queen.

In the midst of a ruined land, a revenant prince returns to claim what is his by birthright. With a small but overwhelming force, he inches his way to his throne. The reigning queen resists- but for how long can she hold on? The cards are laid bare on the table, but neither side is willing to show their ace.

Amongst the conflict, a wildcard emerges. A child appears out of nowhere in a small town, recalling no memory but their own name. With an unsatiable desire, they ravage the land indiscriminately in search of their identity.

Interdiction is a Pokémon fangame with the following features:

  • 800+ brand new, hand-drawn sprites for all Pokémon up to Generation 7
  • 60+ new compositions, along with 10+ additional songs courtesy of GlitchxCity
  • Completely new tileframes and world assets
  • A new dimension of battling to explore in Ultima Evolution
  • Revamped RNG system
